How it works

Friction you can feel, in three seconds.

nofocus doesn't lock sites away. It adds a short, deliberate pause — long enough to break the autopilot.

01

You open a blocked site

Instead of the page, nofocus shows you a plain mirror screen. No shame, no pop-up nagging.

02

You see the count

How many times you've tried today, how long since the last time, and one short line. Then: close, or open anyway.

03

You earn your way in

Still want in? Type one sentence and wait out a short countdown. The site unlocks for a set time, then locks again.
